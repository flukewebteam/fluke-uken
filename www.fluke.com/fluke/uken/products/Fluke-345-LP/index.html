The Fluke 345


Troubleshooting modern electrical loads requires a powerful new breed of tool. The Fluke 345 combines unprecedented measurement capabilities with incredible portability. It’s a clamp meter, an oscilloscope, a data logger, and a digital power meter all in one. What’s more, it’s easy to use and extremely safe. Welcome to the future of power quality.
