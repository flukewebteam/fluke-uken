Clamp Meters: Readings You Can Rely On

Wires in tight compact spaces. Panels out of reach. Extra-large conductors. We understand your workspace and have designed products for noise-free, reliable readings. Built on success, clamp meters designed by Fluke are known as workhorse clamp meters. With advanced performance, the digital clamp meter line is designed to be simple to use and gimmick-free.

And now key Fluke clamp meters can share results with Fluke Connect® wireless test tools. Take a reading, send it remotely to a Fluke Connect master unit, and you're ready to send measurement data to your smartphone, save and share measurements from the field with your team, at anytime, from anywhere.

Fluke is the world’s most trusted name for clamp meters. The extensive Fluke range includes models that feature wide opening jaws for safe, fast non-contact current measurement. The ergonomic design of Fluke clamp meters are ideal for measuring current in even the most confined of spaces.

Expanded models for current measurement

The Fluke digital clamp meters come in a wide variety of models. Ranging from the small bodied 320 Series to the more robust and rugged 370 series for industrial and utility applications.

The clamp meters allow measurement of the electric current in the conductor without having to make physical contact with it, or to disconnect it for insertion through the probe. Fluke’s clamp meters offer flexibility and user friendliness with easy to use products and large display screens.

Simply choose the product that fits your work or click on "View all" to view every digital clamp meter.
