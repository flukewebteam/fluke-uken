Thermal imagers built for the toughest industrial environments


Get the thermal imagers that are built on 65+ years of industrial experience. Each camera is built without compromise to the Fluke standard of "ruggedness, reliability and accuracy". Designed for everyday use in any environment, for thorough and accurate inspections. Choose from the affordable and versatile Performance Series, the Professional Series that offers superior image quality, or the Expert Series that gives you up to 1024x768 resolution on the industry's largest screen.

A Performance Series thermal imaging camera offers good image quality at an affordable price. With up to 320x240 resolution, you can see more detail in every image, even from a distance - detail that could indicate a potential issue. With a rugged design and easy-to-use user interface, these cameras are ideal for troubleshooting tasks. Every model comes standard with Fluke Connect® and a smart battery with LED charge indicator, and most models include a removable SD card and an adjustable robust hand strap.

A Professional Series thermal camera gives you in-focus, crisp, detailed images. Get clear, accurate focus throughout the field of view with MultiSharp™ focus (Ti480 and Ti450), or focus with laser speed and accuracy on your designated target with LaserSharp® Auto Focus. Every model comes standard with Fluke Connect®, advanced documentation and viewing features, and a high resolution touchscreen LCD, so you can clearly see what you are measuring and quickly navigate the menu. Measure high temperatures, up to 1200 °C (2192 °F). Add an optional telephoto or wide angle lens for extra detail from near or far.

The Expert Series infrared cameras produce extremely detailed images, and with the large 5.7" LCD displays, you get a premium viewing experience. With the >240° articulating lens, you can navigate over, under and around objects to capture those tough shots with ease. Measure high temperatures up to 1200 °C (2192 °F), and take advantage of the most advanced focus options available for consistently in-focus images, including MultiSharp™ Focus – focus near and far throughout the field of view; LaserSharp® Auto Focus – instant focus on your designated target, and manual focus. Every model comes standard with Fluke Connect. Add an optional IR lens for extra detail from near or far.
