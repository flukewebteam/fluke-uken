Fluke 365 Clamp


The Fluke 365 offers a smaller, detachable jaw with three feet of cord. The small jaw gives you access to tightly packed wires, while the detachable function allows you to easily read the display, which is especially helpful when dealing with high or hard-to-reach places. Capable of measuring to 200 A ac or dc, the 365 delivers advanced signal processing for accurate and repeatable readings.

Small jaw, big idea.
  • Smaller jaw with detachable head
  • 200 A ac and dc current measurement
  • 600 V ac and dc voltage measurement
  • True-rms ac voltage and current
  • 6000Ω resistance measurement
  • Large, easy-to-read backlight display
  • Built-in flashlight
  • Compact design
  • CAT III 600 V safety rating
