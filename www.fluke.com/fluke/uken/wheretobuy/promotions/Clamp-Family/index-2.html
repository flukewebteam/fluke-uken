Clamp Family


Our new family of clamp meters provides a range of state-of-the-art features to meet the most demanding job requirements.

Our 365 model offers a smaller, detachable jaw, while four other models can use our flexible current probe, called the iFlex™, to squeeze between tightly packed wires or around large conductors. The 381 features a detachable display that easily allows for remote measurements. Five of our models are CAT IV rated, and all are durably constructed. Four models can attach to the truly revolutionary iFlex, with its 18-inch flexible coil and six feet of cord. Fluke clamps are designed for you—rugged, reliable, professional. Choose the clamp that meets your needs.

Fluke 365 » 
The family's base model offers everything you need in a day-to-day clamp meter. A true-rms meter rated to 200 A ac, it is as durable as it is competitively priced.

Fluke 373 » 
The family's base model offers everything you need in a day-to-day clamp meter. A true-rms meter rated to 600 A ac, it is as durable as it is competitively priced.

Fluke 374 » 
With all of the features of the Fluke 373, the Fluke 374 measures dc current, is iFlex compatible with inrush and min/max/avg readings. A true-rms meter, the 374 is CAT IV 600 V, CAT III 1000 V rated.

Fluke 375 » 
Taking everything the 373 and 374 offer, the Fluke 375 also comes with increased resistance range, frequency to 500 Hz, mV dc and integrated low pass noise filter.

Fluke 376 » 
The 376 provides increased measurement capabilities up to 1000 A and 1000 V ac and dc, and includes the iFlex flexible current probe that allows you to take current measurements in crowded cabinets, between tightly packed wires or around large conductors. CAT IV 600 V, CAT III 1000 V rated.

Fluke 381 » 
Combines iFlex flexibility with remote reading capability. The detachable digital display allows you to attach the meter in one place and take the reading in another, up to 30 feet away. CAT IV 600 V, CAT III 1000 V rated.
