Fluke 381 Clamp


The Fluke 381 Clamp Meter combines iFlex flexibility with remote reading capability. The iFlex flexible current probe measures large conductors and provides access in tight spaces. The detachable digital display allows you to attach the meter in one place and take the reading in another, up to 30 feet away. It's like being in two places at once. CAT IV 600 V CAT III 1000 V rated, the Fluke 381 combines innovation with safety.
