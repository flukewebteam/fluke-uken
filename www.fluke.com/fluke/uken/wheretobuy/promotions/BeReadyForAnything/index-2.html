Introducing the Fluke 376 True-rms AC/DC Clamp Meter with iFlex™.


The Fluke 376 with iFlex is our most advanced clamp meter yet. iFlex is a flexible current probe that allows you to take current measurements in crowded cabinets, between tightly packed wires or of large conductors that don't fit within a typical clamp jaw. The 376 is able to measure 1000 A ac and dc with the jaw and 2500 A ac with the iFlex. CAT IV 600 V rated, the 376 delivers accurate and stable readings because of its advanced signal processing and low pass filter and is over-molded to provide exceptional durability. Plus, Fluke features you've come to depend on: Large, backlit display, auto power off, ergonomic design. In short, innovation meets safety in a truly flexible clamp.

The Fluke 376 with iFlex: Now nothing is beyond your reach.
